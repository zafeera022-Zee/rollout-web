Party games

UNO

A card game where players match colors or numbers while using action cards to defeat their opponents.

CHARADES

A party game where players act out words or phrases without speaking while others guess.

TWISTER

A physical party where players place their hands and feet or colored spots without falling.

MAFIA

A sosial deduction game where players use teamwork and deception to identify the hidden mafia members.

TABOO

A word guessing game where players describe a word without using the forbidden clues.

CODENAME

A team word game where players give clues to help teammates identify secret agents without choosing the wrong cards.
