Family Games

MONOPOLY

Property trading game where players buy, sell and develop properties to bankrupt their opponents.

LUDO

Classic race game where players roll dice to move their pieces and be the first to reach the finish.

SNAKES AND LADDERS

Luck based board game where players roll dice to move forward while climbing ladders and avoiding snakes.

SCRABBLE

Word game where players create words using letter tiles to earn points.

CLUEDO

A mystery game where players solve the crime by finding the suspect, weapon and location.

JENGA

Skill based game where players remove and stack blocks without making the tower fall.
