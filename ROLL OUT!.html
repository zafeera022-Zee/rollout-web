ABOUT

What is Roll Out?

Imagine touching grass... but with board games. Roll Out! is a one day event where fun, chaos and questionable alliances happen over dice, cards and way too much trash talk.

Mission

To remind everyone that the best multiplayer experience doesn't always need a loading screen.

Why people should come

  • Try games you probably haven't played before.
  • Meet people who are just as competitive as you.
  • Make friends. Make enemies. Rematch later.
  • Take aesthetic photos for your socials.
  • Snacks + games = peak vibes.
