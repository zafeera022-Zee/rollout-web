Strategy Games

CHESS

A two player strategy game where the goal is to checkmate your opponent's king.

CHECKERS

A two player game where players move pieces diagonally to capture all of their opponent's pieces or block them from moving.

RISK

A board game where players compete to conquer territories and achieve world domination.

PANDEMIC

A cooperative board game where players work together to stop global disease outbreaks and save humanity.

CHINESE CHESS

The goal is to checkmate your opponent's general using unique pieces and rules.

CARCASSONNE

A tile placement strategy game where players build landscapes and earn points by creating cities, roads and fields.
